FRC Team 4919 2019 Robot Design

Saturday January 5th schedule (6:00am to 5:00pm) for Leodroids went to Kearny High School for Kick-Off video and to receive Kit of Parts for selected students. After the video, students at Kearny High School picked up the Kit of Parts and then returned to LCHS. Students at LCHS helped by setting up the room with about eight tables and chairs and assist with preparing getting the manual printed and distributed to the tables. Once all the students were back at LCHS, we discussed the video and then broke up in to groups to break down the manual for the rest of the day and started to discuss options.
